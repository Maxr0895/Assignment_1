You need to find your Cognito Domain in the AWS Console:

1. Go to: https://ap-southeast-2.console.aws.amazon.com/cognito/v2/idp/user-pools
2. Click on your User Pool: ap-southeast-2_9tnsorRRj
3. Go to "App integration" tab
4. Scroll down to "Domain" section
5. Copy your domain (it will look like: xxxx.auth.ap-southeast-2.amazoncognito.com)

=============================

Then add these lines to your .env file:

COGNITO_USER_POOL_ID=ap-southeast-2_9tnsorRRj
COGNITO_CLIENT_ID=60oqtchvn8lb55rmo6olr0h9f9
COGNITO_DOMAIN=<YOUR_DOMAIN_HERE>.auth.ap-southeast-2.amazoncognito.com
COGNITO_REDIRECT_URI=http://localhost:8080/callback
COGNITO_LOGOUT_URI=http://localhost:8080/logout
SESSION_SECRET=super-secret-session-key

=============================

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Find your Cognito domain (see above)
2. Open your .env file in Cursor
3. Add the 6 lines above (with your actual domain)
4. Save the file
5. Run: npm run dev
6. Visit: http://localhost:8080/login
